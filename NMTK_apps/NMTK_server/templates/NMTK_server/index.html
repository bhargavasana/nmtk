    The US Federal Highway Administration Non-Motorized Toolkit (NMTK) is a tool designed to facilitate the planning of non motorized forms of transportation through the implementation and application of transportation models using region specific data. Transportation planning tools may be provided by a variety of entities (commercial, education, government, etc.) and integrated into the toolkit via a well-defined API.

    The toolkit can then utilize these tools using user-provided data and user-specified model parameters to execute a variety of transportation models (tools.) These results may then be viewed (and downloaded) using the Toolkit server.

    This product is currently in development, and is not currently available to the public.
